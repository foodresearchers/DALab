Message from the Supervisor

Dr. Maruf Ahmed - "I am happy to welcome you to our lab. Generally, people prepare, process, or consume food in a variety of ways, whether it comes from plants, animals, fungi, or other sources. Currently, food shortages are significant issues globally. Innovation and problem-solving in the food sector are essential for ensuring that food products are safe, of high quality, and flexible to customer and environmental needs. Consequently, this lab plays a vital role in the food sector, developing eco-friendly food processing methods, extending shelf life, augmenting nutritional content, waste management, and satisfying customer requirements. Besides this, the lab also focuses on food microbiology and manufacturing the equipment that is used in the food industry. This lab will serve as a bridge between the scientific understanding of food and its practical application, ensuring that the global food supply is safe, high-quality, and capable of meeting the demands of a growing population. "

About Us

Dr. Ahmed Lab is dedicated to advancing the field of food research through innovative and groundbreaking studies. Our team of experienced researchers and scientists work tirelessly to explore new frontiers in food technology, nutrition, and safety.

At our lab at Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science and Technology University (HSTU), we delve into the captivating world of food science, exploring innovative techniques to enhance the quality and safety of what we eat. Our team is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of modern food processing, with an emphasis on unlocking the potential of bioactive compounds. Through our research, we aim to contribute to the advancement of food technology and nutrition, paving the way for healthier, more sustainable food solutions.

Our lab is a hub of creativity and innovation, where researchers collaborate to solve complex problems and make impactful discoveries. We are passionate about making a difference in the field of food science and beyond.
